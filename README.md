# Privacy Policy for HealthCal - Calorie Tracker

Last updated: April 14, 2026

HealthCal - Calorie Tracker ("HealthCal", "we", "our", or "us") is a general wellness app that helps users track calories, meals, weight, steps, and sleep. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app accesses, how it is used, and the choices available to you.

## Summary

- HealthCal reads step count and sleep data from Apple Health only if you grant permission.
- HealthCal stores your profile, calorie plan, meal logs, and weight logs on your device.
- HealthCal can send food search queries to USDA FoodData Central when you use food search.
- HealthCal does not require an account.
- HealthCal does not currently include third-party advertising or in-app analytics SDKs.
- HealthCal is intended for general wellness and lifestyle tracking, not for medical diagnosis or treatment.

## Information We Access

HealthCal may access the following information depending on how you use the app:

### 1. Apple Health Data

With your permission, HealthCal reads:

- Step count
- Sleep analysis data

This data is used to show activity and sleep summaries inside the app and to generate wellness-oriented insights such as daily scores or nudges.

HealthCal does not write health data back to Apple Health.

### 2. Information You Enter

HealthCal stores information you choose to provide, including:

- Name
- Age
- Biological sex
- Height
- Current weight
- Target weight
- Activity level
- Goal or intent, such as weight loss, maintenance, or muscle gain
- Meal and food log entries
- Weight log entries

This information is used to create your calorie plan, show your progress, and support app features you request.

### 3. Food Search Queries

If you use food search, HealthCal may send your search terms to USDA FoodData Central to retrieve food and nutrition results.

## How We Use Information

HealthCal uses information to:

- Show your dashboard, logs, and trends
- Calculate your calorie target and nutrition plan
- Display meal history and food details
- Show steps and sleep summaries if Apple Health access is granted
- Generate general wellness insights and habit-focused nudges
- Improve app reliability and basic functionality

## How Information Is Stored

- Profile and calorie plan information is stored locally on your device.
- Meal logs and weight logs are stored locally on your device.
- Apple Health data is accessed through Apple Health permissions and displayed in the app.
- Food search requests are sent over the network only when you actively search for foods.

At this time, HealthCal does not require a user account and does not operate a customer cloud account system for syncing your data across devices.

## Third-Party Services

HealthCal currently uses the following external service for food search:

- USDA FoodData Central: used to search for food items and retrieve nutrition details when you search within the app

When you use USDA-backed food search, your query is sent to USDA FoodData Central. The USDA may receive technical information that normally accompanies internet requests, such as IP address and request metadata, according to its own practices and policies.

## Health Data Commitment

If you allow HealthCal to access Apple Health data:

- Health data is used only to provide app features you request.
- Health data is not used for advertising.
- Health data is not used for marketing.
- Health data is not sold to third parties.

## Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information.

We do not share your health data with data brokers or advertisers.

We may disclose information only:

- If required by law
- To protect rights, safety, or prevent fraud
- In connection with a business transfer, if one occurs in the future

## Your Choices

You can control your data in several ways:

- You can deny or revoke Apple Health permissions at any time in Apple Health and iPhone Settings.
- You can delete meal log entries from within the app.
- You can reset your profile and calorie plan from within the app.
- You can remove the app from your device.

If you want full deletion of locally stored app data, you can also delete the app from your device, which typically removes app sandbox data stored on that device.

## Children's Privacy

HealthCal is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## International Users

If you use HealthCal outside your home country or region, your food search requests may be processed by external services in other jurisdictions depending on the service infrastructure used.
